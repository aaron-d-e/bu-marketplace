Policy & Agreement

Last updated: March 2026

1. User Eligibility

S.H.A.R.E Bear is exclusively available to current Baylor University students, faculty, and staff. Users must have a valid @baylor.edu email address to register and participate in the marketplace.

2. Account Responsibilities

Users are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of their account credentials. Any activity conducted under your account is your responsibility. Report any unauthorized access immediately.

3. Listing Guidelines

All listings must comply with the following:

  • Items must be legal to sell in the State of Texas
  • No prohibited items (weapons, alcohol, tobacco, drugs, or controlled substances)
  • Accurate descriptions and honest representation of item condition
  • Reasonable pricing that reflects fair market value
  • Original photos of the actual item being sold

4. Prohibited Conduct

Users may not:

  • Post false, misleading, or fraudulent listings
  • Harass, threaten, or discriminate against other users
  • Use the platform for commercial business purposes
  • Circumvent platform fees or policies
  • Share or sell account access

5. Transaction Safety

All transactions should be conducted in safe, public locations on campus. S.H.A.R.E Bear recommends meeting at designated campus exchange points during daylight hours. Never share personal financial information through the platform.

6. Content Ownership

Users retain ownership of content they post but grant S.H.A.R.E Bear a license to display, distribute, and promote listings within the platform.

7. Dispute Resolution

S.H.A.R.E Bear provides a platform for peer-to-peer transactions but is not a party to any sale. Users are encouraged to resolve disputes directly. For unresolved issues, contact our support team for mediation assistance.

8. Account Termination

S.H.A.R.E Bear reserves the right to suspend or terminate accounts that violate these policies without prior notice.

9. Policy Updates

These policies may be updated periodically. Continued use of the platform constitutes acceptance of any changes. Users will be notified of significant policy changes via email.

Questions?

If you have questions about these policies, please contact us at sharebear@baylor.edu.
