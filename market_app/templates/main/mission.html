Our Mission

S.H.A.R.E Bear Mission Statement

S.H.A.R.E Bear is dedicated to fostering a sustainable campus community at Baylor University by providing a trusted marketplace where students can buy, sell, and share goods.

Our Vision

We envision a campus where sustainability meets community, creating a circular economy that benefits all Baylor students while reducing waste and promoting responsible consumption.

Our Values

  • Sustainability: Promoting reuse and reducing campus waste
  • Community: Building connections among Baylor students
  • Trust: Creating a safe, verified marketplace for campus commerce
  • Accessibility: Making affordable goods available to all students
