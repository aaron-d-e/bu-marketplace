Our Mission

S.H.A.R.E Bear Mission Statement

At S.H.A.R.E Bear (Sustainable Holistic Affordable Reusable Essentials), we believe no usable item should end up in a landfill. We connect Baylor University students with affordable, pre-loved dorm essentials — promoting sustainability, reducing waste, and building community one item at a time.

Our Vision

We envision a campus where sustainability meets community, creating a circular economy that benefits all Baylor students while reducing waste and promoting responsible consumption. Through our platform, students can buy, sell, and donate items, keeping resources in circulation and making college life more affordable and environmentally responsible.

Our Values

  • Sustainability: Promoting reuse and reducing campus waste
  • Community: Building connections among Baylor students
  • Trust: Creating a safe, verified marketplace for campus commerce
  • Accessibility: Making affordable goods available to all students

What S.H.A.R.E Stands For

S - Sustainable
H - Holistic
A - Affordable
R - Reusable
E - Essentials
