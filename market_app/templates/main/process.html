How it Works

Selling your item to the Baylor Marketplace is simple. Here’s the process from quote to payment.

  1. 1

    Get your quote

    Complete a free AI assessment to get an accurate quote on what we can give you for your item.

  2. 2

    Upload your video

    If you’re happy with the price, take a video of your item and upload it to the website so an administrator can approve and review your product.

  3. 3

    Schedule pickup

    After that, you’ll hear from a representative to schedule a pickup for your item.

  4. 4

    Get paid

    Once we validate your quote, you receive funds electronically—and you’re done!

  5. 5

    We list it

    We then process and post your item on the marketplace for buyers to purchase. All proceeds go back to Baylor and help sustain the marketplace, providing students with affordable, high-utility goods.
